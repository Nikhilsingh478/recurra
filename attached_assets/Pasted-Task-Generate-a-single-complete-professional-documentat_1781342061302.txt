Section 14 вҖ” Known Limitations
Be honest. Document these exactly:

Rate limit bypassable вҖ” IP-based limiting is not foolproof. A VPN bypasses it trivially.
In-memory rate limiting вҖ” The Map resets on serverless cold starts. Under high traffic with multiple instances, limits may not be enforced consistently. A Redis-backed store would fix this.
Frequency count accuracy вҖ” Gemini may slightly miscount frequencies across many papers. The dynamic priority system compensates for minor inaccuracies, but exact counts are not guaranteed.
localStorage dependency вҖ” Results are stored in localStorage. Clearing browser storage or switching devices loses the results. No server-side persistence.
Free tier Gemini limits вҖ” On the free tier, gemini-2.5-pro is limited to 50 requests per day across all keys. Heavy usage requires a paid API plan.
PDF rendering accuracy вҖ” The paginateUnits() height estimation is approximate. Long questions with LaTeX may occasionally cause minor page overflow.
